Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our medical and laboratory equipment services.

What is the difference between an AMC and a CMC?

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) covers scheduled preventive-maintenance visits and labour. A Comprehensive Maintenance Contract (CMC) covers the same visits and labour and additionally includes spare parts, for more complete cost cover.

How often should medical equipment be maintained and calibrated?

It depends on the device type and the manufacturer's guidance. Most clinical and laboratory equipment is serviced on a quarterly to annual preventive-maintenance schedule, with calibration typically carried out annually or as required by your accreditation body.

What does an electrical safety test (EST) cover?

An electrical safety test checks earth/ground continuity, insulation and leakage currents on mains-powered medical equipment, and provides a documented pass/fail result to support compliance and audits.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and facilities across the United Arab Emirates.

How do I request a service visit or a quotation?

Contact us by phone or through the contact form with your facility type and the equipment involved, and we will respond to arrange a visit and provide a tailored quotation.

What do you provide after a service visit?

You receive a service report, and where applicable a calibration certificate and validity sticker, to support your accreditation requirements and audits.

Do you supply spare parts?

Yes. We source and supply quality replacement parts to keep your equipment running, either on their own or as part of a comprehensive maintenance contract.
